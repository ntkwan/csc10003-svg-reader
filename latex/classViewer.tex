\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a viewer for rendering and interacting with a scene. 

The viewer supports the following interactions\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Rotation\+: Press \textquotesingle{}Q\textquotesingle{} to rotate the view counterclockwise and \textquotesingle{}E\textquotesingle{} to rotate clockwise.
\item Zooming\+: Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out of the scene.
\item Translation\+: Click and drag the left mouse button to translate the view. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
